What I do

Development

The process of developing a website is exciting, rewarding, and fun. The website is brought to life through the use of fun animation and creative design that has to ability function through multiple browsers.

Responsive

You can save time and money by creating a website using RWD that serves all platforms vs building and developing multiple designs for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Animation

An animated, interactive website is more likely to attract and entice users. Using the languages JavaScript, Jquery, and CSS3, elements of the website can move, float, slide, etc.

Creative

Whether it’s inventing concepts for my Fantasy Football app or thinking of ways to make my current projects one of a kind, I am continuously brainstorming new, innovative ideas that are useful to the design/development process.
